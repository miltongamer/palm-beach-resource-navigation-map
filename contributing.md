# Contributing

Thank you for considering contributing to the Palm Beach Resource Navigation Map.

This project is designed to become a practical, maintained resource for people helping homeless adults, veterans, families, and people facing behavioral health, substance use, legal, housing, food, transportation, and crisis-related barriers in Palm Beach County, Florida.

## Ways to contribute

You can help by:

- Adding verified local resources
- Correcting outdated contact information
- Confirming hours of operation
- Adding eligibility requirements
- Improving referral pathways
- Flagging inactive programs
- Adding source links
- Improving plain-language descriptions
- Suggesting new categories or fields

## Resource quality standards

Please prioritize official or reliable sources:

1. Organization website
2. County or government website
3. VA or official federal source
4. Direct phone or email confirmation
5. Recent official PDF or directory
6. Trusted partner confirmation

Avoid adding resources based only on old directories, social media posts, or unverified third-party listings unless clearly marked as needing verification.

## How to add or update a resource

When adding or updating a resource, include as many of the following as possible:

- Name
- Category
- Description
- Phone number
- Website
- Address or service area
- Population served
- Eligibility notes
- Referral process
- Hours
- Cost/payment information
- Transportation availability
- Source URL
- Last verified date
- Verification status
- Confidence level

## Tone and language

Use clear, practical, nonjudgmental language. The project should be usable by both professionals and families trying to help someone quickly.

Avoid jargon when possible. When clinical, legal, or government terms are needed, explain them simply.

## Safety and emergency information

Do not present this project as a substitute for emergency services, clinical judgment, legal advice, or crisis intervention.

For immediate danger or life-threatening emergencies, call 911. For suicide or mental health crisis support in the United States, call or text 988.

## Pull request checklist

Before submitting a change, check that:

- The resource is relevant to Palm Beach County or clearly serves Palm Beach County residents.
- A source is included.
- The information is written clearly.
- The verification status is accurate.
- The update does not include private personal information.
- Any crisis, medical, legal, or eligibility information is cautious and source-based.
